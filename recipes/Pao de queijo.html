Pao de queijo

Ingredients:

  • (400g) Tapioca flour 2/3 cup
  • (320ml) Milk
  • (100g) butter
  • 2 Eggs, large
  • 1 teaspoon Salt
  • 85g Grated mozzarella cheese or any other cheese
  • 50g Parmesan cheese, grated

How to make:

Mix solids then liquids, which cheese being the last 200c 10m-15m depends on the size
