Cinnamon Rolls with frosting

Ingredients:

  • 140 g (1 cup) Self Rising Flour (OR 1 cup all purpose flour + 1 1/2 tsp baking powder + pinch of salt)
  • 70 g Milk ( 1/4 cup + 1/2 tbsp Milk)
  • 40 g Softened Butter ( 3 tbsp )

    • Cinnamon Roll Filling:

  • 30 g melted Butter ( 2 tbsp)
  • 50 g Brown Sugar (1/4 cup)
  • 1/2 tbsp Cinnamon
  • 1/2 tbsp Cornstarch
  • Pinch of Salt

How to make:

Mix solids then liquids, roll in a thin sheet, the thinnest you can, and 1m microwave.
