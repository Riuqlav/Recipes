Banana bread

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 40 grams of sugar
  • 1 sachet of baking powder/chemical yeast
  • 150 grams of flour
  • Sliced bananas on top cut for looks
  • baking sheet

How to make:

Mix solids then liquids, pour on the form on top of the baking sheet 180c 40m-45m depends on the color
